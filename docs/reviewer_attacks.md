# Reviewer Attacks

- This is just shadow segmentation with different words.
- The experiments are synthetic, so the claim may not transfer to real robots.
- Reflections are rarer than shadows; the combined state may be over-parameterized.
- Prior self-modeling papers already capture body geometry, so adding illumination may be incremental.
- If the method does not improve a downstream task, the paper becomes a perception-only curiosity.
